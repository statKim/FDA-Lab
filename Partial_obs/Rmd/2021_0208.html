Simulation settings

Lin, Z., & Wang, J. L. (2020). Mean and covariance estimation for functional snippets. Journal of the American Statistical Association, 1-13.

  • 5 \(\text{cov I}, n=200, \delta=0.25, \text{SNR}=2\) , \(N=100\)
  • outlier (outlier 0.2 )
  • Variance \(\sigma_X^2\) covariance \(C\) estimation performance

Outliers settings

  • Outlier 1~3
    • Outlier wiener process mean function ( gaussian process)
    • 3 mean function ( \(\mu(t) = 2t^2\cos(2\pi t)\))
      • \(\mu_1(t) = -2t^2 \cos(2\pi t)\) (\(y\) )
      • \(\mu_2(t) = -3\sin(4\pi t)\) (fluctuation )
      • \(\mu_3(t) = 2(t-0.2)^2\cos(2\pi(t-0.2))\) (\(x\) )
  • Outlier 4~6
