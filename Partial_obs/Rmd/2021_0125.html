Performance measure

  • Variance estimation \[\begin{aligned} \text{RMISE} &= \sqrt{ \frac{1}{N} \sum_{i=1}^N \int_{\mathcal{T}} |\hat{\sigma}^2_X(t) - \sigma^2_X(t)|^2 dt} \\ \text{RMSE} &= \sqrt{ \frac{1}{N} \sum_{i=1}^N \frac{1}{n(\mathcal{T})} \sum_{t \in \mathcal{T}} |\hat{\sigma}^2_X(t) - \sigma^2_X(t)|^2} \end{aligned}\]
  • Covariance estimation \[\begin{aligned} \text{RMISE} &= \sqrt{ \frac{1}{N} \sum_{i=1}^N \int_{\mathcal{T}}\int_{\mathcal{T}} |\hat{C}(s,t) - C(s,t)|^2 dsdt} \\ \text{RMSE} &= \sqrt{ \frac{1}{N} \sum_{i=1}^N \frac{1}{\{n(\mathcal{T})\}^2} \sum_{t \in \mathcal{T}} \sum_{s \in \mathcal{T}} |\hat{C}(s,t) - C(s,t)|^2} \end{aligned}\]
