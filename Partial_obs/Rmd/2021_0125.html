Validation measure

  • Variance estimation \[\begin{aligned} \text{RMISE} &= \sqrt{ \frac{1}{N} \sum_{i=1}^N \int_{\mathcal{T}} |\hat{\sigma}^2_X(t) - \sigma^2_X(t)|^2 dt} \\ \text{RMSE} &= \sqrt{ \frac{1}{N} \sum_{i=1}^N \frac{1}{\mathcal{T}} \sum_{t} |\hat{\sigma}^2_X(t) - \sigma^2_X(t)|^2} \end{aligned}\]
  • Covariance estimation \[\begin{aligned} \text{RMISE} &= \sqrt{ \frac{1}{N} \sum_{i=1}^N \int_{\mathcal{T}}\int_{\mathcal{T}} |\hat{C}(s,t) - C(s,t)|^2 dsdt} \\ \text{RMSE} &= \sqrt{ \frac{1}{N} \sum_{i=1}^N \frac{1}{\mathcal{T}^2} \sum_{t} \sum_{s} |\hat{C}(s,t) - C(s,t)|^2} \end{aligned}\]
